How the Visualization Works

38 events across 3 meaningful phases

PHASE 1: Appear

Events 1-19

19 clusters fade in at their geometric positions

2.5s per cluster

PHASE 2: Transition

Events 20-30

Internal state swap (instant, not animated)

~0.1s each

PHASE 3: Merge

Events 31-37

Clusters implode toward combined centroid

5s per merge

PHASE 4: Final

Event 38

All clusters converge to center

8s total

Design Decisions & Future Plans

Why we made these choices and what's next

Key Design Decisions

Decision Reason
Hard-coded positions GTSfM folders don't encode 3D data
Removed "lock in" animation No visible change - just confusing
Slower animations (3-5s) Let viewers see full transition
Animation completion check Don't advance until done
Auto-tracking camera Always see the action
